# Business Insights

The dashboard enables business users to answer questions such as:

- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- Which products generate the highest profit?
- Which customer segment contributes the most sales?
- Which regions underperform?
- Which cities have the highest sales?
- How has sales performance changed over time?
- Which customers should receive loyalty incentives?

---

## Sample Insights

- Technology products generated the highest revenue.
- Consumer customers contributed the largest percentage of sales.
- Abuja recorded the highest sales among cities.
- Laptop sales accounted for a significant share of revenue.
- Profit margins remained positive across all regions.
